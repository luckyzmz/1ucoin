1ucoin Draw Mechanism:
- Dynamically splits any mainstream cryptocurrency (price > 1 USDT, e.g., BTC, ETH, BNB, SOL, XMR, LTC, BCH, AAVE..) into 1 USDT shares using Chainlink Price Feeds.
- Users purchase shares via USDT on Polygon.
- Fair draw conducted via Chainlink VRF.
- USDT converted to target cryptocurrency via QuickSwap.
- Non-winners receive ODC (1 USDT = 1 ODC, 100 ODC = 1 USDT).
